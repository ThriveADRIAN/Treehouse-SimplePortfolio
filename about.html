About

I am an artist. Technology is my palette. I paint the world I want to see by building products I hope will resonate. If I fail, I learn from it and start my next piece.

I am currently building an organization called ThriveStreams.

Born and raised in the San Francisco Bay area, Adrian currently resides in Brooklyn, NY where he enjoys reading to gain more knowledge, meditating, journaling, exercising (i.e. Yoga and Running), playing the guitar, and exploring the wonders of New York City whether by foot, skateboard, bicycle, subway or taxi. When on vacation or back in California, he loves to surf and snowboard depending on the season. He has a passion for art, music and all things creative.

If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my username is @adriancunanan.
